About us

Welcome to caffetteria, a cozy coffee shop founded by three passionate students who shared a love for coffee and community. Our mission is to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where our customers can indulge in delicious coffee and pastries while enjoying the company of friends and neighbors.

At caffetteria, we believe that great coffee is more than just a drink - it's an experience. That's why we source only the highest quality beans and prepare each cup with care and attention to detail. Whether you prefer a classic espresso or a specialty drink, our menu has something for every taste.

As students, we understand the importance of value and affordability, and we strive to provide our customers with a quality coffee experience at an accessible price point. Our team is dedicated to making your visit a memorable one, and we hope to welcome you back to caffetteria time and time again.

Thank you for choosing to enjoy your coffee with us, and we look forward to serving you soon.
